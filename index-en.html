BYOL 400

ADVANTAGES

  • Speed
  • Durability
  • Practicality
  • Affordability
  • Sustainability

What is it for?

BYOL® 400 is not an anti-adhesive, but a new concept for fast and risk-free welding: like no one else can do! Unlike all other systems, BYOL® 400 is composed only of natural and food-grade ingredients, guaranteeing incredible results. Applied on the torch, it guarantees hours and hours of welding without the need for cleaning. It also increases the life of spare parts.

Why is it different?

The drying time after application is eliminated, it does not contain dangerous elements, it is certified, and can be used with any type of material. It can also be used in laser and plasma cutting, in addition to electrode and induction welding. It can be removed easily in pre-painting processes.

BYOL R

ADVANTAGES

  • Harmless
  • Efficient
  • Conductor
  • Economic
  • Ecological

What is it for?

Product for automatic welding, harmless to humans. Designed to increase the results of any welding robot, any process and on any type of material. Like BYOL® 400, BYOL® R also guarantees a significant increase in productivity and a guarantee in reducing cleaning times, as well as reducing maintenance and spare parts consumption.

Why is it different?

Being a good conductor, BYOL® R leads robots to reduce false triggers and to track the joints to be welded with very high precision. Statistics show an increase in welding of 23 minutes every 8 hours, as well as a reduction in spare parts by around 50 EUR each month per robot.

What is it for?

Multi-purpose oil, suitable for industrial environments (threading, drilling, lubrication and surface protection), and domestic environments (for the bicycle chain, for example. Or the key in the door)

Why is it different?
